Notice of privacy

Laws Jurídicas y Cobranzas, through its website under the domain www.lawsjuridicasycobranzas.com is responsible for the use and protection of your Personal Data, and in this regard we inform you of the incoming:

What information do we collect?

The Personal Data that you provide us when you register as a user of this website, through the use of our online services, in writing and / or by telephone, may be the fincoming:

Name, identifier, place of birth, nationality, address, home phone, cell phone, email, work address, institutional email, work references, tax information, credit history, income, bank accounts, business references, etc.

For what purposes can we use your personal data?

We will use your personal information for the incoming purposes that allow and facilitate us to provide you with better care:

Identification and verification purposes. Contact, marketing and / or advertising. Offer our products and / or services. Participation in promotions and contests. Provide products and / or services required by you or on your behalf. Inform about changes or new products or services offered. Evaluate the quality of the service. Identify consumer antecedents or habits.

With whom do we share your personal data?

We can transfer or share your Personal Data to national or foreign third parties, subsidiaries and affiliates; service providers for promotional and marketing activities of our products and services; or at the request of an authority. It is understood that you have given your full consent to the transfer of your Personal Data, if you do not express written opposition to its transfer.

The Personal Data that you provide us may be collected and set in a database owned exclusively by Laws juridicas y cobranzas, which is not responsible for the content and privacy policies of third companies.

How can you access, rectify or cancel your Personal Data or oppose its use?

You have the right to know what personal data we have about you, what we use it for and the conditions of use that we give it (Access). Likewise, it is your right to request the correction of your personal information if it is outdated, inaccurate or incomplete (Rectification); that we remove it from our records or databases when we consider that it is not being used properly (Cancellation); as well as to oppose the use of your personal data for specific purposes (Opposition). You must submit the respective request through the incoming means:

Send your request to the email: contacto@lawsjuridicasycobranzas.com

You can revoke the consent that, where appropriate, you have given us for the processing of your personal data. However, it is important that you bear in mind that not in all cases we will be able to respond to your request or terminate the use immediately, since it is possible that due to some legal obligation we need to continue treating your personal data. Likewise, you must consider that for certain purposes, the revocation of your consent will imply that we will not be able to continue providing the service you requested, nor the conclusion of any type of relationship with us.

To revoke your consent, you must send your request to the email address: contacto@lawsjuridicasycobranzas.com Remember to accompany your request with a copy of your identity document.

How can you find out about the changes in this privacy notice?

We remind you of the importance of reading this Privacy Notice each time you are going to use our website, as it may undergo modifications.

This privacy notice may undergo modifications, changes or updates derived from new legal requirements; of our own needs for the products or services we offer; of our privacy practices; Changes in our business model, or other causes. We promise to keep you informed about the changes that this privacy notice may undergo, through: the publication on our website.
